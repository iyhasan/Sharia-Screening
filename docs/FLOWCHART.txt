================================================================================
PURIFICATION FORMULA EXPLANATION
================================================================================

When debt ratio >= 33%, calculate purification percentage:

   Purification % = min(10%, max(2.5%, Debt Ratio / 330))

   This means:
   - Minimum: 2.5% (if debt < 8.25%)
   - Linear scale: increases with debt ratio
   - Maximum: 10% (at or above ~33% debt)

   Examples:
   • Debt = 5%    → Purification = 2.5% (minimum)
   • Debt = 16.5% → Purification = 5.0% (mid-range)  
   • Debt = 35%   → Purification = 10% (maximum cap)

================================================================================
METHODOLOGY
================================================================================

Based on AAOIFI (Accounting and Auditing Organization for 
Islamic Financial Institutions) Sharia screening standards, 
widely accepted in Sunni scholarship.

Key principles:
• Prohibited sectors (riba usury/haram businesses)
• Maximum interest-bearing debt allowed
• Ethical business operations required
• Purification of any non-compliant revenue portions
